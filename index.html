Our mission

Every tool on UIForge exists to eliminate a tiny friction point in the development workflow — the moment when you need to generate a gradient, check a contrast ratio, or build a CSS animation and end up copy-pasting from a Stack Overflow answer from 2014.

We believe developer tooling should be instant, opinionated, and completely free. No accounts, no paywalls on the basics, no nagging. Just open the tool and ship.

What we've built

  • CSS Generators — Gradient, box shadow, border radius, animation, clamp, transform, glassmorphism, clip-path, SVG blob
  • Layout Tools — Flexbox playground and CSS Grid generator with live visual feedback
  • Color Tools — Palette generator with harmony modes, HEX ↔ RGB converter, WCAG contrast checker
  • Utilities — Unix timestamp converter, text case converter with 12 naming conventions

Every tool generates real, production-ready CSS you can copy and paste directly into your project. No frameworks required, no output to clean up.

Principles

Speed over features

UIForge is a single HTML file. It loads in under a second on any connection. We deliberately choose simplicity over adding every possible option.

Accessible by default

Every tool meets WCAG 2.1 AA contrast standards. The app itself uses proper semantic HTML, keyboard navigation, and ARIA roles throughout.

No dark patterns

No ads, no tracking pixels, no "sign up to copy" gates. The Pro tier (coming soon) funds development of new tools — it never locks away what's already free.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Your data stays
your business

UIForge is built to respect your privacy. Here's exactly what we collect, store, and why.

📅 Last updated: March 2026
The short version: UIForge runs entirely in your browser. No generated CSS, tool settings, or design choices are ever sent to our servers. We collect only anonymised traffic analytics.

What we collect

Anonymous analytics

We use privacy-first analytics (no cookies, no cross-site tracking) to understand which tools are most used and how pages are discovered. Data collected is aggregate-only — we cannot identify individual users.

Contact form submissions

If you submit our contact form, we receive your name, email address, and message. We use this only to reply to you. We do not add you to any marketing list without explicit consent.

What we do NOT collect

  • The CSS you generate or copy — all processing happens in your browser
  • Your IP address in any identifiable form
  • Cookies for tracking or advertising purposes
  • Any data from third-party social logins (we don't have login)

Local storage

UIForge uses your browser's localStorage to save gradient presets you create. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser storage.

Third-party services

  • Google Fonts — Used to load typefaces. Google may log font requests per their privacy policy
  • No advertising networks — UIForge does not serve ads or use ad-tech tracking
  • No social media pixels — No Facebook Pixel, TikTok Pixel, or similar

Your rights

Under GDPR and similar regulations, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold. Since we hold almost none, simply contact us and we'll respond within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as UIForge evolves. Changes are posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

Contact
